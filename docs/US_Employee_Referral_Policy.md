# Employee Referral Program

**MONSANTO COMPANY**  
**Policy Number:** 206  
**Human Resources Policy Manual**  
**Date:** November 2005

---

## Scope

This policy applies to all U.S. Monsanto employees.

---

## Purpose

The purpose of this program is to promote employee referrals of qualified candidates to secure regular full-time employment for approved, difficult to fill openings within Monsanto.

To encourage, recognize and reward Monsanto employees who refer qualified external candidates who are hired for approved, difficult to fill openings.

---

## Policy

The following describes the Monsanto Employee Referral Program:

### i) Eligible Positions

Referral awards will only be paid for approved, difficult to fill job openings. Each opening will be evaluated by the Hiring Manager, Recruiter and the Human Resources Generalist to determine eligibility.

A special notation will be added to the intranet job posting for openings that are eligible for a referral award.

---

### ii) Eligible Employees

All regular Monsanto employees (including regular full-time & regular part-time) are eligible to participate in the program, **except:**

- Human Resources Personnel or employees who are primarily responsible for employee recruitment (i.e., career fair attendees on behalf of Monsanto).
- Any employee with influence over the hiring process or hiring decision for a particular position.
- M04 People Managers and above.
- Persons who are not regular Monsanto employees (i.e., contractors, temporary employees, interns/co-ops, consultants, RRC employees, etc.).
- Persons who are employees of Monsanto joint ventures/subsidiary companies that do not participate in standard Monsanto policies and compensation plans.

---

### iii) Eligible Candidates

Referral Candidates may **not** be:

- a. Temporary, contract, former/current Monsanto employees, interns/co-ops
- b. RRC employees
- c. Represented by an employment agency
- d. Employee transfers
- e. Referral candidates applying online after the approved posting has been removed from the internet

> **Note:** For any referrals of employee relatives, please see the policy, *"Hiring and Placement of Relatives."*

---

### iv) Payment of Award

Referral award payments will be paid after the referred employee's first successful **90 days of employment**. This payment is taxable income and is subject to appropriate tax reporting and withholding.

Both the referring employee and the new employee must be active employees at the time the award payment is scheduled.

Hiring businesses/departments will be charged for the referral award payment.

Only one referral award shall be paid for each referred candidate hired. If more than one employee refers the same candidate, the first referral received will be eligible for payment.

There is no maximum to the number of referral awards for which an employee can be eligible.

---

### v) Award Levels

Referral awards will be paid via the next available payroll cycle and awards are not eligible for SIP and Pension. The award amounts will be defined depending on the level of the position being filled.

| Type of Position | Referral Award Amount |
|---|---|
| Entry to Intermediate-Level (LV1 – M03) | $500 |
| Mid-Level to Senior Level (M04 – M06) | $1,500 |

---

### vi) Funding

The program is funded at the local level. Costs will be paid out of the hiring department's budget.

---

## Procedure

- To view eligible openings, please visit the career opportunities section on the Navigator. Employee referral award information is only on the intranet job posting.
- To email a link for a specific opening to a prospective referral, please visit that opening on [www.monsanto.com](http://www.monsanto.com).
- The referring employee is responsible for directing the candidate to the Monsanto website ([www.monsanto.com](http://www.monsanto.com)) to submit a complete CV/resume through the normal application process. The candidate must note **Employee Referral** as the source and then enter the referring Monsanto employee's full name in the space provided.
- Staffing Specialists will process the referral award as part of the New Hire Data Form and Payroll will process the payment after 90 days of employment (see section iv: Payment of Award).
- Each referred candidate will be considered for any Monsanto job opening and may be hired for a position that does not have an award amount attached to it.

---

> **NOTE:** Monsanto reserves the right to change conditions and award amounts or cancel the program entirely. Monsanto reserves the right to consider which candidates to pursue and to make a final hiring decision. All information regarding the reason(s) for making a decision not to hire an applicant will remain strictly confidential.

*Thank you for all of your Employee Referrals!*
